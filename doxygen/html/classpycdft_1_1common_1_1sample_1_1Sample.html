Detailed Description

The physical system to be simulated.

All physical quantities are in atomic unit.

Variables ending with _r are defined on G space grid. Variables ending with _g are defined on G space grid. Variables ending with _rd are defined on radial grid in R space; variables ending with _d correspond to all G vectors with different norm. They are used to compute atomic densities. 

R (np.ndarray, shape = [3, 3]): the real space lattice vectors of the system.
G (np.ndarray, shape = [3, 3]): the reciprocal space lattice vectors of the system.
