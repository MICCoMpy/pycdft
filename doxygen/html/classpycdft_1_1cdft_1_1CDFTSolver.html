Detailed Description

Constrained DFT solver.

Vc_tot (float array, shape == [vspin, n1, n2, n3]): total constraint potential as a sum of all constraints defined on all fragments.

def pycdft.cdft.CDFTSolver.solve_scf (   self)

Iteratively solve the CDFT problem.

An outer loop (implemented below) is performed to maximize the free energy w.r.t. Lagrangian multipliers for all constrains, an inner loop (casted to a KS problem and outsourced to DFT code) is performed to minimize the free energy w.r.t. charge density.
